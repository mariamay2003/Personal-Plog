A guide to discovering paris!

Emma’s Tips for a Perfect Trip

  • Learn a few basic French phrases: Even if it’s just “Bonjour” (Hello), “Merci” (Thank you), and “Excusez-moi” (Excuse me). The locals appreciate it when you make an effort.
  • Use the Metro: It’s the quickest and most efficient way to get around. I used an app called Citymapper, and it made navigating super easy.
  • Try local food: From crepes on the street to escargot at a fancy restaurant, don’t be afraid to try new things.
  • Walk as much as possible: Paris is best explored on foot. You’ll discover charming little streets, hidden gardens, and beautiful architecture that you might miss otherwise.
